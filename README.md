# 🛒 Super Market Sales Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard developed to analyze supermarket sales data and generate actionable business insights through interactive visualizations.

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Screenshot](Screenshot.png)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of supermarket sales performance, helping stakeholders understand:

- Total Income
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Customer Rating
- Sales Distribution by City
- Sales by Payment Method
- Product Line Performance
- Gender-wise Income Contribution
- Product Line Ratings

## 📈 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Income | 31.39K |
| Total Quantity | 11K |
| Average Rating | 7.20 |

## 📌 Dashboard Features

### 1. City-wise Sales Analysis
- Compare total income across different cities.
- Identify top-performing locations.

### 2. Payment Method Analysis
- Analyze customer payment preferences.
- Compare Cash, E-Wallet, and Credit Card transactions.

### 3. Product Line Performance
- Revenue contribution by product category.
- Product-wise average ratings.

### 4. Customer Insights
- Gender-based income contribution.
- Customer satisfaction analysis through ratings.

## 🎯 Business Insights

- Food & Beverages generated the highest income.
- Cash payments were the most preferred payment method.
- Male customers contributed a larger share of total income.
- Fashion Accessories received the highest average customer rating.

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Download the repository.
2. Open `Dashboard.pbix` using Power BI Desktop.
3. Refresh the dataset if needed.
4. Explore the interactive dashboard visuals.
